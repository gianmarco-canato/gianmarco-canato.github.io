Liminal Duo

Liminal Duo was founded in 2021 by Gianmarco Canato (bassoon) and Mattia Olgiati (Electronics) with the intention of merging their personal musical languages and exploring the interaction between the acoustical instrument and the vast sonic possibilities of the electronics.

The sound of the bassoon is captured, processed and transfigured to the end of expanding its expressive capabilites, crossing its physical limits, incontrovertibly creating an unforeseeable stimulating mix of languages, one which reinterprets typical styles of the traditional repertoire. The collaboration between the two musicians is not limited to performance only, but expanding the repertoire with original works is among their goals, along with collaborations with other artists and musical improvisation.
